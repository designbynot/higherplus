0001: Ethereum

Idea of Ethereum

A world computer. For the people, by the people. But bigger and better.

Mental model: Ethereum is bringing Massive Multiplayer Online Role Playing Games (MMORPGs) to the real world

  • A global, high-stakes, computer game but it's your real life
  • Global economy
  • Open world

Style of Ethereum

Mainnet is relatively neutral. It's Layer 2s that make opinionated and centralized style decisions.

Ethereum is part science fiction, part real-time software. It should lean into wireframe aesthetics to bridge both worlds and align w its own Layer 2 roadmap.

Culture & Behaviors

The current culture of ethereum is software developers (micro and macro focused), digital artists (tech leaning), and independent finance (high risk investors, entrepreneurs, and startups).

By digital artist A.C.K

CryptoPunks by Larva Labs (acq. by Yuga Labs)

Base Layer 2 (blockchain) daily transactions

Seed Club (private venture) acquisition of Build

Symbol

The symbol of Ethereum is the Ethereum Crystal.

0002: $ANON

Recommendation for Anon Scalers

  • Idea: Anonymity
  • Style: Black and White
  • Culture & Behavior: Cyberpunk. Anarchism. Degeneracy.
  • Symbol: ?

First Hand Design Sprint

After running a design sprint on the account myself. It became evident that this would be one of the most powerful, censorship resistant, chaotically neutral memecoins to afford digital space to anyone who wanted to embrace the more primitive aspects of our industry.

  • Say what you really want to say - to power, to your network, to yourself, etc
  • Embrace anonymity technology like the original cypherpunks desired
  • Create art, software, and media without the constraints of external judgement
  • Rebel for a larger purpose or for the entertainment of it all
